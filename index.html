About us

Cipher Educational Consult Limited is a premier educational consulting firm registered in Nigeria. We specialize in bridging educational gaps and providing comprehensive services, including international student admissions, tutoring, research services, and more, aimed at empowering students across Nigeria and West Africa.

Our services are designed to support students throughout their educational journey, offering everything from personalized academic counseling to international admission assistance and visa processing.

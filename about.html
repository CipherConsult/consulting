Executive Summary

Cipher Educational Consult Limited is a premier educational consulting firm registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in Nigeria. Incorporated on 22nd November 2023, with registration number RC 7234606, we specialize in providing comprehensive educational services, consultancy, international student admission services, admission preparation, tutoring, and research services. Our mission is to bridge educational gaps and offer opportunities for academic and personal growth to students across Nigeria and West Africa.

Mission

Driven by a commitment to empower and uplift, Cipher Educational Consult Limited seeks to bridge the educational divide, particularly for those affected by insurgency in northeastern Nigeria. Our founders, intimately acquainted with the challenges, aspire to showcase the resilience and talent of the region to the world. At the heart of our mission is the Educational Sponsorship program, designed to empower deserving students from northeastern Nigeria.

Vision

Shaping Futures, Guiding You To Global Educational Excellence. To be a leading educational consultancy that transforms lives through education, creating pathways to success for students worldwide.

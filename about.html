Executive Summary

Cipher Educational Consult Limited was founded with the vision of making international education accessible to all. Founded by visionaries hailing from the insurgency-ravaged northeastern part of Nigeria. We serve not only as a study abroad agency, but as a catalyst for change. Driven by a commitment to empower and uplift, we seek to bridge the educational divide, particularly for those affected by insurgency in northeastern Nigeria.

Our founders, intimately acquainted with the challenges, aspire to showcase the resilience and talent of the region to the world by channeling the untapped potential of the insurgency-traumatized individuals into the avenues of education and growth with the Educational Sponsorship Program, designed to empower deserving students from northeastern Nigeria.
